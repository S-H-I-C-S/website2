About Us

Resource Development

Create and distribute culturally relevant educational materials for African students and teachers globally.

Teacher Training

Provide continuous professional development to enhance teaching skills and methodologies for African educators.

Educational Advocacy

Advocate for policies ensuring equitable access to quality education for African students worldwide.

Cultural Exchange

Promote cross-cultural collaboration between African educators and global counterparts to enrich learning experiences.

The Association of Global African Teachers is a vibrant and thriving organization dedicated to supporting educators across the globe in their professional development and personal growth. As the Head of this remarkable charity, I, Caroline Ebenuwah, feel privileged to lead a community that is deeply committed to empowering teachers. 

Our charity is passionately focused on creating a nurturing and supportive environment where educators can flourish and unlock their full potential. By providing exceptional resources, tailored support, and a rich array of opportunities, we aim to equip teachers with the skills and confidence they need to make a profound impact on the lives of their students.

The work we do is unashamedly ambitious, striving for excellence in all that we undertake. We offer a wealth of opportunities, including a stimulating curriculum, access to cutting-edge facilities, and a vibrant extracurricular program that enhances personal and professional growth. Our mission is to develop confidence, foster strong professional relationships, and create a community where educators are valued as individuals.
